SOBEK SINGH

THE CREATIVE FORCE

I am Sobek Singh, a filmmaker, creative director, fashion photographer, commercial spec ad creator, and concert photographer with a strong focus on visual storytelling. I have worked at the Khajuraho International Film Festival 2025 and collaborated on podcast productions with renowned artists such as Anupam Kher, Saurabh Shukla, and other notable industry professionals.

My experience includes working with multiple clothing brands as a fashion photographer, as well as conceptualizing and producing spec advertisements for brands like Triumph Bikes and various other creative projects. I also specialize in live music photography, capturing performances by artists such as Arpit Bala, Chaar Diwaari, and many more.

With a diverse portfolio spanning films, branded content, and live events, I continuously strive to expand my creative boundaries and collaborate with a wide range of artists and brands to deliver compelling visual narratives as a creative director and visual storyteller.
